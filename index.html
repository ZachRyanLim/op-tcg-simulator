💡 Gameplay Tips
  • Select a card first, then use the action buttons on the right
  • DON!! the leader before your characters for +1000 power
  • Blocker cards can redirect attacks — watch for the keyword
  • Rush cards can attack the turn they're played
  • End your turn when you have no more actions — DON!! resets each turn
  • Life cards go to hand when damaged — use them strategically
  • Right-click (or hover, if set above) any card to see full details
  • Deck Builder: build 50-card decks, max 4 copies per card
