OPTCG Block Rotation

The Official OPTCG Standard format uses block rotation. Older sets rotate out so the game stays fresh and competitive.

⛔ Rotated Out (Not Legal in Standard)
OP-01 Romance Dawn OP-02 Paramount War OP-03 Pillars of Strength OP-04 Kingdoms of Intrigue
✅ Legal in Standard Rotation
OP-05 to OP-15 All ST Starters EB-01 Extra Booster PRB-01/02 Premium
When Block Rotation is enforced, cards from rotated sets are marked illegal in the deck builder and excluded from games. This matches the Official Standard Tournament format.
